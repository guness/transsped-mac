## TransSped v0.0.2

A **native app window** replaces the old one-shot dialogs. Opening TransSped now
shows a real status window instead of a prompt that flashes and closes.

**Signed with a Developer ID and notarized by Apple** — the DMG opens on any Mac
with no Gatekeeper warning.

### What's new

- **Native window** — first run shows a **Set up** card (enter your userID);
  once set up, a grouped status panel shows **Installed in Firefox**, your
  **account**, and the **certificate's expiry** (amber when it's within 30 days).
- **Actions in the window** — **Open ANAF login**, **Update certificate**,
  **Uninstall**, and **About**, with a clear primary/secondary hierarchy.
- **Firefox-open notice** — a clear callout when Firefox needs to be quit before
  Update/Uninstall.
- Under the hood, the setup logic is now a headless engine
  (`Contents/Resources/tscloud-engine`) the window drives — also runnable from
  scripts.

Login itself is unchanged: pick **Trans Sped Cloud** in Firefox, enter **PIN +
OTP** (tick **Remember PIN** to skip the PIN next time).

### Install

1. Download **`TransSped.dmg`** below and open it.
2. **Quit Firefox**, then run **TransSped** (from the DMG, or drag it to
   Applications first).
3. Enter your **Trans Sped userID** (the email/phone for your cloud certificate)
   in the Set up card. The app fetches your certificate and registers a PKCS#11
   module into your **normal** Firefox profile.
4. Open Firefox → your ANAF login → **certificate** method → **Trans Sped
   Cloud** → **PIN + OTP**.

### Requirements

- macOS 13 or later
- Firefox (Chrome/Safari can't load a PKCS#11 module)
- A Trans Sped **cloud** qualified certificate already enrolled with ANAF (form 150)

### Uninstall

Open TransSped and click **Uninstall** (with Firefox quit) — it removes the
module from Firefox and deletes `~/.config/tscloud` (including any remembered PIN).
