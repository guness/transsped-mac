## TransSped v0.0.3

**Romanian language support** 🇷🇴 — the app and the sign-time PIN/OTP dialog are
now fully localized in Romanian and English.

**Signed with a Developer ID and notarized by Apple** — the DMG opens on any Mac
with no Gatekeeper warning.

### What's new

- **Română + English**, throughout: the window (setup, status, buttons, About)
  and the **PIN + OTP dialog** shown inside Firefox at login time.
- **Language menu** — a globe (🌐) in the window's header: **System · English ·
  Română**. It switches instantly.
- **Defaults to Romanian** when your Mac's language is Romanian (otherwise
  English); your choice is remembered and also governs the sign-time dialog.
- Dates in the status panel follow the chosen language's locale.
- Now **MIT-licensed** (© 2026 Sinan Gunes) — shown in the **About** panel.

Everything from v0.0.2 (native window, notarized DMG) is unchanged; login itself
works the same — pick **Trans Sped Cloud** in Firefox, enter **PIN + OTP** (tick
**Remember PIN** to skip the PIN next time).

### Install

1. Download **`TransSped.dmg`** below and open it.
2. **Quit Firefox**, then run **TransSped** (from the DMG, or drag it to
   Applications first).
3. Enter your **Trans Sped userID** (the email/phone for your cloud certificate).
   The app fetches your certificate and registers a PKCS#11 module into your
   **normal** Firefox profile.
4. Open Firefox → `https://www.anaf.ro/` → certificate login → **Trans Sped
   Cloud** → **PIN + OTP**.

### Requirements

- macOS 13 or later
- Firefox
- A Trans Sped **cloud** qualified certificate already enrolled with ANAF (form 150)

### Uninstall

Open TransSped and click **Uninstall** / **Dezinstalează** (with Firefox quit).

See the [README](https://github.com/guness/transsped-mac/blob/main/README.md)
for details.
